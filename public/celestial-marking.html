Birth date (for stillbirth/infant death), estimated conception date (for miscarriage), or loss date. Even approximate timing works for reading.

No. Evolutionary astrology is a psychological and spiritual framework, not a religion. It works alongside any faith tradition or no tradition.

That's okay. Many clients are curious but skeptical. We can do meaningful memorial work with or without the chart reading. Your choice entirely.

How long does chart reading take?
60-90 minutes typically. We cover your baby's chart, soul contracts, cosmic timing, and how this informs memorial tattoo design.
