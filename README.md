## Key Service Areas

### 1. Memorial Work (Primary Focus)

**Target Population**: Bereaved parents experiencing pregnancy loss, stillbirth, infant death, or failed fertility treatments

**Statistics**:
- 1 in 4 pregnancies end in miscarriage
- ~1-1.5 million miscarriages annually in the US
- ~24,000 stillbirths annually
- 65-70% of IVF cycles fail

**Service Model**: Integration of bereavement doula support with therapeutic memorial tattoos

**Key Benefits**:
- Creates permanent connection where temporary existence existed
- Makes invisible loss visible
- Supports continuing bonds with deceased
- Reduces shame and isolation
- Provides sense of control in grief journey

**Mobile Tattooing**:
- Varies widely by state/locality
- Some states prohibit (Maine)

- Guest artist arrangements at licensed studios (easiest compliance)

**Doula Services**:
- Mostly unregulated (no state licensing required)
- Virtual doula care proven effective (2024 research)
- California, New York, Minnesota Medicaid covers telehealth doula services

**Financial Viability**:
